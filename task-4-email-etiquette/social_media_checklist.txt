SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’TS

DO’S: 1. Use a professional profile picture. 2. Keep your personal
information limited and secure. 3. Think before posting anything online.
4. Respect others and communicate politely. 5. Verify information before
sharing.

DON’TS: 1. Do not share passwords with anyone. 2. Avoid posting
offensive or inappropriate content. 3. Do not accept friend requests
from strangers. 4. Avoid oversharing personal details like address or
phone number. 5. Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
