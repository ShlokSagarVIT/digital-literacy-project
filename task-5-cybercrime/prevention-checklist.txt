STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

1.  Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.
2.  Do not scan QR codes to receive money – QR codes are used to pay,
    not receive.
3.  Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or
    companies.
4.  Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links received via SMS or
    email.
5.  Use official apps only (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm) and keep them
    updated.
6.  Enable app lock and two-factor authentication on your devices.
7.  Regularly check your bank statements for unauthorized transactions.
8.  Do not install unknown apps from unofficial sources.

IMPORTANT: • Report cyber fraud immediately at:
https://cybercrime.gov.in • Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
(available 24x7)
